Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2026

Mielo ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Mielo platform, accessible at heymielo.com and app.heymielo.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

By using Mielo, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: your name, email address, restaurant name, and settings you provide when creating an account.
  • Review data: reviews from your connected platforms (Google, TripAdvisor, Yelp) that you choose to manage through Mielo.
  • Usage data: how you interact with the Mielo platform, including pages visited and actions taken.
  • Google account data: when you connect your Google Business Profile, we access your business reviews and publish responses on your behalf. We do not access any other Google account data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Generate and publish responses to your customer reviews
  • Provide insights and analytics about your review performance
  • Improve the quality of AI-generated responses over time
  • Send you notifications about new reviews and account activity
  • Provide customer support

3. Google API Services

Mielo uses Google API Services to access your Google Business Profile reviews. Our use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Specifically, we only use Google data to:

  • Read reviews left on your Google Business Profile
  • Post responses to those reviews on your behalf

We do not sell, share, or use your Google data for any other purpose.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, hosted in the European Union. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Mielo:

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Anthropic — AI response generation (review content is processed to generate responses)
  • Stripe — payment processing (we do not store your payment details)
  • Vercel — hosting and infrastructure

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with third parties as necessary to operate the service (listed above). We may disclose data if required by law.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Disconnect your Google account at any time
  • Export your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@heymielo.com.

8. Cookies

Mielo uses essential cookies to keep you logged in and maintain your session. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

9. Children's Privacy

Mielo is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or through the app. Continued use of Mielo after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
